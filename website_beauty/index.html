Dermotherap Gun

Applications Exclusive benefits Technical specifications

Applications

This equipment is ideal for body, facial and hair mesotherapy treatments. In the application of botulinum toxin, both in facial wrinkles and in plantar, axillary and palmar hyperhidrosis. Also its use is excellent in sclerotherapy.

It is also used for: "PRP" (Platelet Rich Plasma)

Valid for multiple procedures (mesotherapy, sclerotherapy, infiltration with botulinum toxin, others.).

Exclusive benefits

Among the unique benefits of this revolutionary invention, it can be noted that it does not leak between punctures, the dosage is correct, the needle used does not get contaminated and it uses any syringe; it is an extremely lightweight device, can be used in multiple aesthetic techniques and the relationship between their costs and benefits is excellent.

Technical specifications

DTP Gun is an automated mesotherapy gun.

It allows to know the exact dose applied in each shot.

No wiring during use, because it works with rechargeable battery.

Digital controls

Accurate automated dosage. It does not lose liquid between injections, due to its bidirectional advance system.

It works with any type of 5 cc luer lock syringe and needle (there is no dependence on specific kits).

Extremely light (15.87 oz).

With full battery charged it can operate 12 hours.

Depth regulator of application of the needle on the skin (See how this is said at the medical level)
